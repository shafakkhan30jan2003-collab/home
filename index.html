About AI College

S.S. College, Jehanabad, is one of the leading institutions of higher education in the district. It is affiliated with Magadh University, Bodh Gaya, and offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce. The college was established with the aim of providing quality education to the students of Jehanabad and nearby areas.

The institution has a dedicated team of teachers who focus not only on academics but also on the overall personality development of students. Along with classroom teaching, students are encouraged to participate in debates, seminars, cultural events, and sports. The college also organizes social awareness programs and NSS activities to develop values of discipline and responsibility. S.S. College has a library with a good collection of books and journals and laboratories for practical learning. Over the years, the college has produced many successful alumni who are serving in different fields. S.S. College, Jehanabad, truly stands as a center of knowledge and opportunity for the youth.
